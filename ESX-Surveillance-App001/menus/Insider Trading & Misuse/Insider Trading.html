📘 What is Insider Trading?
The act of trading securities based on material, non-public information that gives an unfair advantage over other market participants.

Categories include:
  • 🧑‍💼 Executive Management of the issuers
  • 🏛️ Members of Board of Directors
  • 💼 Major Shareholders (≥ 5%)
  • 👨‍💻 Trading Member Staffs – including traders, compliance officers, advisors, and senior management
  • 👪 Major familiar relationships among the above stakeholder categories
